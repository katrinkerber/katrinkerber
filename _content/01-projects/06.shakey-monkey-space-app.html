--- title: Shakey Monkey Space App status: live intro: |

The Cog Design team were bored by the trend of very similar, 'style-over-substance' poster designs and album covers which appeared in countless magazines, websites, and student portfolios. So they decided to make an App for designers to create their own artwork with a shake.

It went down well with fellow designers like Designer Dan and Creative Inspiration.

Unfortunately, the app is no longer available to download.

goal: '

A fun-poking app that randomly puts together carefully crafted landscape, space, line, shape and animal graphics.

' involvement: '

With the help of the powerful Appcelerator Titanium SDK, I built the app using the languages I know and love best: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

' special: |
  • Over 1 million possible combinations
  • The finished poster is held by a mysterious designer friend
  • Export or share your creations on Twitter

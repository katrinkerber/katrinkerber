--- title: Artichoke intro: '

The Artichoke Trust organises stunning, large-scale events and festivals in unusual places across the country, with the aim to make magical arts accessible and enjoyable to the wider public.

' goal: "

Artichoke's website needed to communicate their stunning work through a variety of media formats, attract donations and keep users up-to-date about current and upcoming events.

" involvement: '

Working with the Cog Design team, I led the build of the site using Expression Engine, Structure, XHTML, CSS and jQuery. I also built a set of easy-to-use Campaign Monitor templates for regular email updates.

' special: |
  • CMS-editable event galleries which can contain images, video and audio
  • fully functional blog
  • small-scale merchandise shop with products linked to PayPal

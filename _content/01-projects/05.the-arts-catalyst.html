--- title: The Arts Catalyst status: live intro: '

The Arts Catalyst commissions unique art projects that fuse art and science in thought-provoking and boundary-expanding ways.

' goal: '

Since 1994 The Arts Catalyst has commissioned over 90 projects and their website needed to convey the depth and breath of their comprehensive library in an exciting way.

' involvement: '

Working with the Cog Design team, I led the build of the site using Expression Engine, XHTML, CSS and jQuery, Google Maps API and sIFR.

' special: |
  • a vast project library filterable by date, title, themes and location, and a quick search functionality
  • easy-to-add project images, which are automatically cropped into zoomable thumbnails
  • randomly highlighted projects across all pages

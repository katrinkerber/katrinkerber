--- title: Aware status: live intro: |

AWARE is the only veterinary conservation trust in Zimbabwe. It was set up to get much needed funding for local projects that look after the welfare of wildlife and wildlife habitats.

This website was my first development gig back in 2009. A fantastic opportunity which I still cherish.

goal: |

AWARE needed to communicate its work in Zimbabwe effectively to both veterinarians and the general public. They wanted to display their great in-the-field imagery and move visitors to donate, volunteer and fund equipment.

involvement: |

I ran the entire process of setting up the domain, emails, hosting, to designing and building the site. I also set up a MailChimp account and template, which is used to keep supporters up-to-date.

--- title: Aware status: live intro: |

AWARE is the only veterinary conservation trust in Zimbabwe. It is run by veterinarians and was set up to get much needed funding for projects that look after the welfare of wildlife and wildlife habitats.

This website was my first development gig back in 2009. A fantastic opportunity which I still cherrish and am grateful for.

goal: |

AWARE needed to communicate it's work in Zimbabwe effectively to both veterinarians and the general public. They wanted to display their great in-the-field imagery and move visitors to supporting them by donating, volunteering and funding equipment.

involvement: |

I ran the entire process of setting up the domain, emails, hosting, to designing and building the site. I also set up a MailChimp account and template, which is used to keep supporters up-to-date.

special: |
  • Linked to Surreal CMS for easy editing of content and adding new pages
  • Easy online donation set-up through PayPal

--- title: Community Foundation Network intro: '

The CFN brings together charitable Community Foundations from all over the UK, to strengthen and improve the lives of local communities.

' goal: '

CFN needed a user-friendly site which could hold the vast amount of information within an easy-to-digest format and structure. The new site highlights the great achievements of community foundations all over the country and effectively talks to philanthropists.

' involvement: '

Working with the Cog Design team; I built the site using Expression Engine, HTML5, CSS, SlidesJS and Mapbox. I also trained CFN members how to update the site and gave support after the launch.

' special: |
  • CMS managed site navigation with the ability to add related pages and success stories to each page
  • Bespoke jQuery map with CMS editable pins and information
